Trust & Protection

Built with Safety in Mind

ONEXO protects every user with intelligent systems that run quietly in the background — so you can focus on connecting.

Fake Email Protection

Email verification ensures every account belongs to a real person before they can post.

Strong Password Check

Password strength is verified at signup to prevent weak credentials from day one.

Login Attempt Lock

Repeated wrong login attempts automatically lock access to prevent brute-force attacks.

Admin User Control

Admins can review, manage, and take action on any account directly from the panel.

Temporary Ban

Users who violate guidelines can be temporarily suspended while a full review takes place.

Permanent Restriction

Fake or harmful accounts can be permanently restricted by admins to protect the community.

13+ Age Restriction

Date of birth is verified at signup to ensure only age-appropriate users can join ONEXO.

Clear Error Messages

Every error is written in plain language so users always know what happened and what to do next.

Platform Management

  • Set how often ads appear in the feed
  • Set ad duration and expiry
  • Enable or disable blue verified badge
  • View users sorted by latest login date
  • Temporarily ban users
  • Permanently restrict fake accounts
